The Best Movies According to Mbulelo

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

It teaches us the meaning of finding and defining our own purpose and self-accept ourselves.

Gladiator

It's an unorthodox love story, a tale of survival against insurmountable odds, an action film, an exploration of the political situation in Rome, and a historical epic.

Enemy at the Gates

Tense and violent WWII movie. While it does have some solid moments of action, the film as a whole is laughable. There's a decent movie buried in here somewhere, but the lousy love triangle and ultimate lack of focus make this WWII drama a bit hard to take.
